Sine Wave Compilation

What can music become
when the only sound available
is a sine wave?

Concept

This project invites musical works created exclusively from sine waves.

By restricting the sound source to the most minimal waveform, the compilation explores the creative possibilities that emerge from extreme limitation.

Why sine waves?

A sine wave is the simplest possible sound.

At the same time, it is the fundamental building block　of all complex sound.

By removing all other sonic materials, this compilation asks a simple question:

What kinds of music remain possible?

Rules

• Sound sources must originate from sine waves.
• Any processing is allowed.
• Be honest to the spirit of the project.

Interpretations of the concept are welcome.
Surprising approaches are encouraged.

Inspirations

Additive synthesis
Algorithmic composition
Minimalism
DSP experiments
Pure tone music

Optional

If you like, include a short description of your process or idea.

Submission

Open worldwide.
Artists of any background are welcome.

Submission deadline
31 July 2026

The compilation will be released on Bandcamp via Hitobito Label.
